# Nimbus People & HR Policies

This document summarizes the people policies at Nimbus Robotics.

## Time off

Full-time employees receive 25 days of paid vacation per year, in addition to
public holidays in their region. Vacation accrues monthly and up to 5 unused
days may be carried into the following year; days beyond that are forfeited at
year end.

## Remote and hybrid work

Nimbus is a hybrid-first company. Engineers are expected in their local office at
least two days per week, on Tuesdays and one self-chosen day. Fully remote
arrangements are possible by exception and require director approval.

## Parental leave

All new parents receive 18 weeks of fully paid parental leave, regardless of
gender or whether the child joined the family by birth or adoption. Leave can be
taken in up to two separate blocks within the first 18 months.

## Learning budget

Every employee has an annual learning-and-development budget of 2,000 US dollars
that can be spent on courses, books, or conference tickets. Unused budget does
not roll over.

## Equipment

New engineers receive a laptop of their choice within a set price band, an
external monitor, and a stipend of 500 US dollars for home-office equipment.

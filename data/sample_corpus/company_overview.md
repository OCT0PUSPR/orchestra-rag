# Nimbus Robotics — Company Overview

Nimbus Robotics is a fictional company founded in 2019 that builds autonomous
warehouse robots and the fleet-management software that coordinates them. Our
flagship product is the **Atlas-7** mobile picking robot, and our coordination
platform is called **Conductor**.

## Mission

Our mission is to make warehouse operations safer and more efficient by letting
robots handle repetitive transport and picking tasks while humans focus on
judgment-heavy work. We measure success by the number of repetitive-strain
injuries avoided across our customer warehouses.

## Headquarters and offices

Nimbus Robotics is headquartered in Portland, Oregon. We operate three
engineering offices: Portland (hardware and controls), Toronto (perception and
machine learning), and Lisbon (the Conductor cloud platform). As of 2026 the
company employs roughly 480 people.

## Customers

Nimbus serves mid-sized third-party logistics (3PL) providers and regional
grocery distributors. A typical deployment ranges from 12 to 200 Atlas-7 units
per warehouse, all coordinated by a single Conductor cluster.

## Funding

Nimbus has raised a total of 145 million dollars across three rounds. The most
recent Series C, closed in 2025, was led by Tidewater Ventures.
